Dungeons n Dice — Pixel Mood Variants

Same pixel-art style and sprites; three environment moods, each with a full light & dark palette. Flip the switch to compare modes. The creatures keep their identity colors (green goblin, red dragon); the mood reskins the dungeon, window chrome, and accents — exactly how the in-app theme system will work (mood × mode).

In the app these become a runtime Light/Dark toggle plus a selectable mood (Settings). Pick the mood you want as the default — all three ship behind the same theme system.

Dungeons n Dice — Style 4: Pixel Dungeon (16-bit)

A classic 16-bit JRPG dungeon-battle look: chunky pixel sprites on a torch-lit stone backdrop, a bordered command/message window at the bottom, a blocky bitmap logo, and a pixel d20 that tumbles in stepped frames. The most genre-native pairing for pixel art — every sprite, item, and the dice are drawn as real pixels (here on canvas; in-app as authored pixel grids).
